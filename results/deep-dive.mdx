## Why it matters

Research reports need context, not just conclusions. The Deep Dive gives you the narrative behind each finding — the exact words people used, the moments where the conversation shifted, the quotes that tell the story.

## What's in it

### By moment

Each topic from your discussion guide gets its own section with:

- A narrative summary of how the discussion went across all sessions
- Themes that emerged within this specific topic
- Key quotes, attributed to specific participants
- Which participants contributed and what they said
- Charts and quantitative breakdowns where relevant
- The moment's [supercut](/results/supercuts) — the strongest video evidence for that topic, playable inline

### By participant

Switch to the Responses view to flip the perspective — see what each person said across every topic, their overall sentiment, and engagement level.

Useful for building participant personas, tracing one person's thinking through the full discussion, or understanding how different segments responded.

## Filtering

Narrow things down by moment, participant, or session. If you're writing up findings for one specific topic, filter to that moment and you get everything you need in one place.
