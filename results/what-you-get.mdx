  You can run analysis after each session or wait until all sessions are done. Analyzing everything together gives you better cross-session insights.
</Info>

## Qualitative depth, quantitative data

Humanize doesn't just give you quotes and themes. It generates quantitative data from your qualitative research — charts, metrics, consensus rates, sentiment breakdowns. You get the depth of a focus group with the data you'd expect from a survey.
